NSF/NCAR GV Hawkeye-CPI Cloud Particle Images
https://data.eol.ucar.edu/dataset/605.023

Summary
Cloud particle images collected with the Hawkeye CPI on board the NSF/NCAR GV
during the Secondary Production of Ice in Cumulus Experiment (SPICULE)
campaign from 29 May through 25 June 2021. These images are available as one
zipped file per research flight.
